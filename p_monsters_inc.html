Monsters, Inc 3D Character

Character inspired by Sulley from "Monsters, Inc."







Date: 2023
Purpose: Modelling for Animation for BSc
Subjects: 3D Modelling, Rigging, Texturing, Rendering, Animation (Posing)
Programs: Maya, Blender

360 View

The Concept

The character creation process drew inspiration from iconic large monster characters, namely Sullivan from Monsters Inc.,Bowser from the Mario games , and the Moblin from The Legend of Zelda. The aim was to design a character with a humanoid posture while incorporating exaggerated physical features that still maintain anatomical coherence. By combining elements from these influential characters, a unique and visually striking character was brought to life, capturing the essence of strength and imposing presence.

Model History

In 2019, I began working on this model as part of a university assignment, using Maya to create the body with appropriate topology for animation purposes. After a few years, I revisited the project and completed the texturing process. I then applied fur to enhance the visual appearance and utilized Blender to rig the model for animation.

The Challenge

Creating an original character with unique features poses significant challenges, particularly when it comes to rigging and posing. The non-standard body structure and distinct features of the character require careful consideration during the rigging process to ensure proper movement and flexibility.

The posing process becomes more intricate due to the unconventional anatomy, requiring custom adjustments and specialized rigging techniques to achieve natural and expressive poses.The success of the project heavily relies on overcoming these challenges, as they contribute to the character's visual appeal and overall believability.

Through meticulous planning and skillful execution, the character's distinctiveness can be leveraged to create a captivating and memorable presence in the game.

Poses

I posed the model to showcase three distinct emotions, meticulously assessing its believability in portraying these specific feelings. Each expression was carefully crafted to reflect the intended emotions, ensuring that they are conveyed convincingly.
