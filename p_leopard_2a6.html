LEOPARD 2A6

A 3D Model of a Leopard 2A6 with camouflage Texture







Date: 2019
Purpose: Game Modelling Fundamentals for BSc
Subjects: 3D Modelling, Texturing and Lighting
Programs: Maya, Blender

The Concept

As part of my assignment, I embarked on the challenging task of accurately modelling the iconic tank, the "Leopard 2A6". This project marked my first foray into the world of 3D creation tools using Maya. With a strong focus on precision and attention to detail, I dedicated myself to crafting a faithful representation of the tank, adhering to the assignment's requirements of modelling without textures.

The Revisit

Years later, inspired by my growth and passion for 3D modelling, I decided to revisit the Leopard 2A6 tank model. Motivated by a desire to elevate its visual appeal, I embarked on the process of adding textures to the model using Blender. This undertaking allowed me to enhance its realism and visual impact.

Through this journey, I experienced the evolution of my skills and gained a deeper understanding of 3D art techniques. The improvement of the Leopard 2A6 tank model stands as a testament to my growth as a 3D artist and my commitment to continuously refine my craft.
